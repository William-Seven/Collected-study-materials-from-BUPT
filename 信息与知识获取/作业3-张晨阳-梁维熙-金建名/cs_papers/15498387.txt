Title: Non-Targeted Screening: A PARC Training Resource

Authors: BAIRA, EIRINI, Termentzi, Aikaterini

Keywords: Chemical Detection, Screening (Chemistry)

Publication date: 2025-05-23

Description:
This training material presents the principles and applications of Non-Targeted Screening (NTS), focusing on Liquid Chromatography coupled with High-Resolution Mass Spectrometry (LC-HRMS). The aim of this presentation is to explore methodological approaches for a broader chemical characterisation, highlighting the importance of harmonised workflows and the emerging role of AI and machine learning in advancing NTS applications.
