Title: Machine learning / Deep learning (ML/DL) calibration scripts to improve S2S monsoon wind speed forecasts from numerical models over India

Authors: Das, Aheli

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-05-07

Description:
<strong>Calibration_scripts.rar</strong>

Contains scripts to calibrate raw S2S 10 m wind speed forecasts using Random forest and Convolutional neural network.

<strong>MMCFSv1_forecasts.rar</strong>

Contains March initialised MMCFSv1 10 m wind speed forecasts of June, July, August, September for the period 1981-2016 over India.
