Title: SOP (Standar Operasional Prosedur) Laboratorium Pendidikan Tata Busana

Authors: Sholikhah, Roudlotus

Keywords: vocational education, fashion laboratory, SOP, technical procedures, TVET

Publication date: 2021-01-01

Description:
This book offers a comprehensive guide to the Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) for Fashion Education Laboratories. It covers essential aspects such as laboratory management, code of conduct, service procedures, equipment and machine usage, and standardized documentation. Designed to support students, lecturers, laboratory technicians, and vocational education stakeholders, this book is an invaluable resource for ensuring safe, efficient, and professional practices in fashion laboratory settings.
