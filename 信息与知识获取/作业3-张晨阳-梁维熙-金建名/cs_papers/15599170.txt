Title: ADVANTAGES AND LIMITATIONS OF LEARNING LANGUAGE THROUGH ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENT

Authors: Kurbanova Dilshoda Kamilovna

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-06-05

Description:
<em><span lang="EN-US">By providing individualized, easily available and adaptable tools, artificial intelligent has completely transformed language acquisition. It provides learning opportunities worldwilde by addressing conventional issues like low participation and delayed feedback. However, because of its limits in emotional intelligence, cultural sensivity, and technological dependence, AI cannot completely replace human educators. This article examines the benefits and drawbacks of artificial intelligent in language acquisitions and suggests a sensible AI with conventional techniques. </span></em>
