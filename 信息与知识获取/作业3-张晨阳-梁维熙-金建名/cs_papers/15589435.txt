Description:
<span lang="EN-US">ESL education for students with speech and language disorders is one of the important areas aimed at alleviating the difficulties they encounter in the process of language learning and ensuring effective learning. Speech and language disorders can manifest themselves in various forms, for example, difficulties in pronunciation, slowness in speech, grammatical errors, limited vocabulary, or problems in speaking and listening. Such disorders complicate language learning for students, therefore, it is necessary to develop special approaches and teaching methodologies for them.</span>
