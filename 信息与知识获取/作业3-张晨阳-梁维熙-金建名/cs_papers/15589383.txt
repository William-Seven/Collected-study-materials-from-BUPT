Title: Hubungan Antara Gaya Belajar dengan Motivasi Murid Pintar Berbakat

Authors: Mohamad Amin, Ahmad Fuad, Ezalazman, Maryam Aisyah, Azharin, Dini Nur Arissa, Khairul Jaza, Dhiya Sarah

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2023-12-26

Description:
This study explores the relationship between learning styles and the motivation of gifted students. Conducted at Kolej PERMATA Insan, USIM, it involved 54 high-IQ Muslim students and employed the Grasha-Riechmann Learning Style Inventory and YDEKC Motivation Scale. Results showed a strong link between collaborative learning and high motivation, highlighting the need to tailor educational approaches for gifted learners.
