Title: Addressing teacher evaluation appropriately

Authors: Lavigne, Alyson, Good, Thomas

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2020

Description:
Education Practice Brief: Given the importance of teachers, there has been intense interest in improving and evaluating teachers. Yet, many current measures of teaching effectiveness are flawed. They fail to adequately account for the content, complexity, and the variance in teacher effectiveness and practice. Because of this, teacher evaluations often yield erroneous information about the quality of teaching and student learning and do nothing to enhance student achievement.
