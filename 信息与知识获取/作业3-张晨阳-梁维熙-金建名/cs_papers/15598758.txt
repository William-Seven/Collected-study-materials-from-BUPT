Title: IMPROVING THE METHODOLOGY FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF PROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION SKILLS OF STUDENTS

Authors: Khilola Sayfiddinovna Turakulova

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-06-05

Description:
<em><span lang="EN-US"><span>&nbsp;</span>This article explores enhanced methodologies for developing professional communication skills among students in higher education. By integrating active learning strategies, technology-enhanced tools, and real-world simulations, the proposed approach aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. The study analyzes existing literature, proposes a mixed-method framework, and evaluates its effectiveness through qualitative and quantitative measures. Results indicate significant improvements in students&rsquo; confidence, clarity, and adaptability in professional settings. Recommendations include incorporating interdisciplinary collaboration and continuous feedback mechanisms.</span></em>
