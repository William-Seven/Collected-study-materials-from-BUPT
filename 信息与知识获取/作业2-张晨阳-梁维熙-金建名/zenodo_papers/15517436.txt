Title: De l'intime au numérique : Étude des témoignages de violences sexuelles dans le mouvement #MeToo sur Twitter en France

Authors: Le Gallic-Ach, Clara

Keywords: violences sexuelles, méthode sociologique, numérique, témoignages, #metoo

Publication date: 2023

Description:

Apr&egrave;s une revue de litt&eacute;rature pluridisciplinaire soulignant les enjeux des t&eacute;moignages num&eacute;riques sur les violences sexistes et sexuelles, une m&eacute;thodologie empirique a &eacute;t&eacute; mise en place. --------


The central question of this research is: how can the digital testimonies shared as part of #Metoo in France enrich our understanding of sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV), by exploring victims' speech, their relationship to their experience, and the tension between the intimate, the collective and the public?


Following a multi-disciplinary literature review highlighting the challenges of digital testimonies on sexual and gender-based violence, an empirical methodology was put in place. This is based on the analysis of over 330,000 tweets mentioning hashtags linked to the #Metoo movement, collected over five years, both longitudinally and retrospectively. The approach includes statistical methods, textual analysis models, temporal analyses, as well as qualitative analyses to identify and examine the content of the testimonials. Finally, the results are compared with different sources of national statistics and media content to situate the specificities of digital narratives in relation to other reporting spaces.
