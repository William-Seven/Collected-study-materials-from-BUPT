Title: Research Software Discovery: How do we Want to Search Research Software and Where do we Want to Find it?

Authors: Wittenborg, Tim, Gey, Ronny, Karras, Oliver, Mietchen, Daniel, Struck, Alexander

Keywords: Research Software, Discovery

Publication date: 2025-05-26

Description:

The workshop aimed to identify current challenges in research software discovery and propose actionable improvements. Using a World Caf&acute;e discussion format, participants explored topics such as the effectiveness of current discovery systems, metadata classification, hosting infrastructures, and AI integration. The workshop generated concrete recommendations for enhancing&nbsp;research software discovery, benefiting developers, researchers, and institutions.
