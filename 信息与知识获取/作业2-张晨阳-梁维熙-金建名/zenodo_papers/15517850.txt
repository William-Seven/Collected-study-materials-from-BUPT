Title: IDIOMS IN BUSINESS COMMUNICATION: A LEXICOGRAPHICAL AND FUNCTIONAL ANALYSIS

Authors: Atayeva Gulchekhra Bakhtiyorovna

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-05-26

Description:

<em><span lang="EN-US">This article explores the diverse nature of idioms, particularly within the context of business English. It examines various classifications of idioms, drawing upon established linguistic theories and focusing on their semantic, structural, and functional characteristics. The article also delves into the lexicographical treatment of idioms, analyzing their representation in dictionaries and highlighting the challenges of capturing their nuanced meanings and usage in bilingual contexts. Furthermore, it emphasizes the creation of a bilingual electronic online dictionary and an interactive didactic online platform dedicated to business idioms, as a valuable resource for language learners and professionals.</span></em>
