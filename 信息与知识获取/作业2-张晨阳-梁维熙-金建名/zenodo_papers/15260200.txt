Title: Dataset for: A web-based app for sunn pest species recognition (Hemiptera: Scutelleridae: Eurygaster) using machine learning

Authors: Neimorovets, Vladimir, Konstantinov, Fedor

Keywords: 

Publication date: 2025-04-22

Description:

Keywords: convolutional neural networks, pests, taxonomy, automated species recognition


Desription:&nbsp;This dataset was used in a project focused on training a convolutional neural network (CNN) for expert-level identification of <em>Eurygaster</em> species. It comprises 5,072 dorsal habitus images of specimens that were reliably identified by expert taxonomist V. Neimorovets from the collection of the Zoological Institute, St. Petersburg. We prepared 48 image sets&mdash;separately for males and females of each <em>Eurygaster</em> species (see Table 1)&mdash;using a variety of imaging equipment, including a DSLR camera (<a name="_Hlk199099902"></a>Canon 50D), a compact camera (Olympus TG-5 with and without flash), and a smartphone (Redmi Note 9 Pro):


<em>Eurygaster</em><em> austriaca</em><em>&nbsp;</em>(Schrank, 1776)


<em>Eurygaster</em>&nbsp;<em>dilaticollis</em> Dohrn, 1860


<em>Eurygaster</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>integriceps</em>&nbsp;Puton, 1881


<em>Eurygaster</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>laeviuscula</em>&nbsp;Jakovlev, 1886


<em>Eurygaster</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>maura</em> (Linnaeus, 1758)


<em>Eurygaster</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>testudinaria</em> (Geoffroy, 1785)


&nbsp;


Likewise, we generated 12 sets of dorsal habitus images using Canon 50D&nbsp;only for each sex of the following true bug species, which resemble <em>Eurygaster</em> spp., to serve as an &ldquo;outgroup&rdquo; for CNN training. These species belong to the families Scutelleridae and Pentatomidae:


<em>Aelia acuminata</em> (Linnaeus, 1758)


<em>Dolycoris</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>baccarum</em> (Linnaeus, 1758)


<em>Holcostethus</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>strictus</em> (Fabricius, 1803) (= <em>Holcostethus</em><em> vernalis</em> (Wolff, 1804))


<em>Odontotarsus</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>purpureolineatus</em> (Rossi, 1790)


<em>Piezodorus</em><em>&nbsp;</em><em>lituratus</em> (Fabricius, 1794)


<em>Staria </em><em>lunata</em> (Hahn, 1835)


&nbsp;


All images were organized into a total of 60 archive files to facilitate downloading (see Table 1). The abbreviations used in the archive names are as follows:


***_Canon50D &ndash; images were taken with a DSLR camera Canon EOS 50D equipped with&nbsp;Sigma 105mm f/2.8 macro lens and Yongnuo YN24EX Macro Flash;


***_Olympus_Flash &ndash; images were taken with an Olympus Tough TG-5 with its built-in flash;


***_Olympus_no Flash &ndash; images were taken with an Olympus Tough TG-5 without its built-in flash;


***_ Olympus_Redmi &ndash; images were taken with a smartphone Redmi Note 9 Pro;


***_M_*** &ndash; sets of males;


***_F_*** &ndash; sets of females.


&nbsp;
